Welcome to TRCHNES – Transforming Waste Management for a Greener Indonesia

Did you know that Indonesia produces over 7.8 million tons of plastic waste every year, with 4.9 million tons left mismanaged? This mismanagement results in waste being dumped into open landfills, incinerated, or worse, polluting our environment through improper disposal methods. Only a fraction—14.58%—of this waste is currently recycled. The rest ends up in landfills or is burned, contributing to harmful air pollution and environmental degradation.

At TRCHNES, we are committed to changing this. Our platform is designed to make waste management easier, more accessible, and more effective for everyone. Whether you're looking to recycle, locate the nearest trash disposal points, or understand how to properly separate your waste, TRCHNES is here to help.
